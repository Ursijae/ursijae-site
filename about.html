About Ursijae

Ursijae is a sustainable clothing brand rooted in San Diego, California. Founded by a husband-and-wife duo, we breathe new life into thrifted denim and vintage fabrics, creating unique patchwork pieces with purpose. Every garment is one-of-a-kind, handmade, and thoughtfully designed to celebrate individuality and conscious consumption.
